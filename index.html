Find National Parks By State

Directions: This app allows you to search for National Parks in one or more states. To search, please enter postal abbreviation(s) for the state(s) in which you wish to find national parks. If you wish to search in more than one state, you must separate each abbreviation with ONLY A COMMA. No spaces should appear in your query.

Examples of correctly formatted queries:

  • CA
  • CA,WA
  • CA,WA,OR

(Results may take several seconds to load.)
